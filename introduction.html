    Introduction to astronomy

    What will you learn?

    In this course on introduction to astronomy, you will gain a basic framework of the universe and its structure. We will explore the field of astronomy, study various celestial objects, dive into the solar system and its planets, and discover some other concepts of universe. After completing the course and passing the exam you will get a free certificate. Do the exam by pressing the "do exam" button and to see the presentation click on "material" button.
