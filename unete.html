    Join us

    At Space Vision, we want to inspire the next generation about space. For this, we have created three categories through which you can participate in Space Vision and motivate future space inspirers.

    To belong to a specific category you only need to fill out a form, and you will be part of our company. These categories are the following:

    Categories

    • Member

      This category is designed for those people who have an interest in space and want to be part of our community. Inspire the new generation in space

    • Space Ambassador

      This category is also designed for all people. The function of the space ambassadors will be to represent the Space Vision company in events, fairs, conferences and scientific outreach activities. As ambassadors they will be asked to share the enthusiasm for space science and showcase the company's projects.

    • Space Engagement Officer

      In this category, the role of people will be to participate in scientific dissemination activities, sharing astronomical knowledge through blogs, talks and videos.
