    About us

    Our goal at Space Vision is to inspire people in space and spread space education worldwide. In addition, we develop projects related to space, offering a unique opportunity to explore and contribute to the knowledge of the cosmos. We seek to inspire and educate people of all ages, from enthusiastic beginners to passionate scientists, by providing easy and accessible access to knowledge and discoveries about space.

    Muneeb Mazhar is the founder of Space Vision, passionate about astronomy and astrophysics. He is sharing the wonder of the universe through inspiring courses.
